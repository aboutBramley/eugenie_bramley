    Studies

    2018 marks my fourth and final year of study at the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire under Emmet Byrne,Philip Haworth and Anna Cooper.
    During my time at the Conservatoire, I have attended and participated in Masterclasses with Francois Leleux, Rachel Pankhurst, Rainer Gibbons, Jill Crowthers, Jonathan Kelly, Philip Harmer, David Walter, Emmanuel Abbühl and Nicholas Daniel.

    Orchestral and Solo Work

    Since I have come to Birmingham (UK) in September 2014 I have performed in various Royal Birmingham Conservatoire Orchestras, in recitals as a soloist and in small ensembles at the Conservatoire.

    I enjoy performing Baroque, Classical, Romantic, Modern repertoire as well as new compositions both on Oboe and Cor Anglais.

    I have participated in projects with The Orchestre national des jeunes de l’Arc Alpin based in Lyon (FRANCE), The CBSO Youth Orchestra in Birmingham (UK) and The Vancouver Symphony Orchestra Institute in Whistler, CANADA.

    I freelance as an oboist and Cor Anglais player in orchestras and ensembles in the Midlands (UK) and beyond.
