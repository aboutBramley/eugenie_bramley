    • As an oboist, I know how important reeds are. I offer an oboe reed service to my students and anyone interested.

    A bit about my reeds…

    Materials

    I mainly use Glotin and K-Ge cain for my reeds, and purchase selected pieces of cain which are already gouged and shaped from a supplier based in Germany.

    To scrape the oboe reeds I use the Reed n’ stuff Udo Deng oboe profiling machine, and I manually finish off the reeds.

    The reeds that I sell don't have any wire on but don't hesitate to ask for some when you place your order.

    Characteristics

    Measurements are personal and depend on what instrument you play on. I have indicated below the specificities of my oboe reeds. Of course I will be very happy to accomodate different measurements for your reeds.

    As an indication, these are the characterstics of my oboe reeds:

    • RC12 shape
    • 46 mm staples
    • 71 mm finished length

    I can make two types of reeds:

    • Soft oboe reeds (recommended for beginners)
    • Medium oboe reeds

    Price

    Each reed has a unique price of 7 GBP. First-class postage fees come in addition.
