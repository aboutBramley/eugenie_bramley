    • Music teaching is a passion of mine. As an oboe and Cor Anglais specialist, I offer private one to one as well as small group lessons of up to three students. I am also happy to teach music in schools and provide music theory lessons.

    Better late than never…

    All ages and levels are most welcome to my lessons.

    Three blind mice to the latest pop tune…

    My ultimate aim is to have fun playing music. I use a wide range of tutor books in my teaching and remain open to new repertoire suggestions. Also, I incorporate Kodaly method and aural training into my lessons to help my students build up a good musicianship.

    Musicians are better together…

    I truly encourage students to join area ensembles to complement their music learning. There is such a great number of diverse ensembles for all levels. I will be more than happy to find the right fit for anyone interested.

    Tutoring for woodwind groups and ensembles is definitely something I truly enjoy.

    My background in musical education includes working for the Ingenium Academy International Summer School for Music and The Royal Birmingham Junior Conservatoire.

    Don’t forget to bow…

    I have in mind to organize a gala concert every year as an opportunity for musical students to perform solo and/or in groups in front of their friends and family in a warm, enjoyable and relaxed atmosphere.
